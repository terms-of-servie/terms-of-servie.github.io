Scrambled Eggs

Here is the recipe for the best scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • One tps of butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. melt Butter over a frying pan medium heat>
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl>
  3. Once butter is melted ass cream and eggs.
  4. using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge to the center of the pan once every 20 seconds
  5. when eggs are still moist remove
